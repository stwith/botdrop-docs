## Download

Go to [botdrop.app](https://botdrop.app) and tap **Download APK**, or get it directly from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/zhixianio/botdrop-android/releases).

<img class="phone" src="/botdrop-docs/images/installation/01-website.png" alt="BotDrop website" />

## Requirements

- Android 10 or later
- At least 2 GB free storage (OpenClaw + Node.js takes ~500 MB)

## Install

Open the downloaded APK. Android will ask you to confirm the installation. Tap **Install**.

<img class="phone" src="/botdrop-docs/images/installation/02-apk-install.png" alt="APK install prompt" />

## Permissions

On first launch, BotDrop asks for two permissions:

### Notifications

Tap **ALLOW** next to Notifications. In the system settings, turn on the notification toggle.

<img class="phone" src="/botdrop-docs/images/installation/03-welcome-permissions.png" alt="Welcome screen" />

<img class="phone" src="/botdrop-docs/images/installation/04-notification-on.png" alt="Enable notifications" />

### Background Running

Tap **ALLOW** next to Background Running. When the system dialog appears, tap **Allow** to let the app run in the background.

<img class="phone" src="/botdrop-docs/images/installation/05-background-dialog.png" alt="Background permission dialog" />

Make sure "Allow background usage" is enabled in battery settings.

<img class="phone" src="/botdrop-docs/images/installation/06-battery-settings.png" alt="Battery settings" />

Once both permissions are granted, tap **CONTINUE** to proceed to [Setup →](/botdrop-docs/setup/).
