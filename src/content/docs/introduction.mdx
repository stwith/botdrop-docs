**Want to install OpenClaw on your phone?** BotDrop makes it one tap. No terminal, no server, no CLI. Download the APK, walk through a guided setup, and your AI bot is live.

## What can it do?

- Run AI agents powered by Claude, GPT, Gemini, and 100+ models via OpenRouter
- Chat with your bot through Telegram, Discord, or Feishu
- Keep it running in the background with auto-restart

## How it works

BotDrop packages [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai/) into an Android app. OpenClaw is an AI agent framework that gives your bot tools, memory, and skills. BotDrop handles all the setup so you don't have to touch a terminal.
